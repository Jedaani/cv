Abstract

A platform for discussing opinions, ideas and fans' reactions to football matches. The platform aims to gather different fans of different teams in one place where they can express what is on their minds at the time of the matches. Each match has its own room in which the fans of the two teams and those who interested in the match meet as well, where they can discuss in writing without dealing with ads or other topics unrelated to the match.
